Welcome!

Do you want to dine in the heart of one of the liveliest cities in the United States, only miles away from various attractions and hot spots?
Do you want to be able to nosh on a crepe one second & an order of our signature wings the next?
Do you want to be able to have endless servings on nearly every single item on our menu without anyone batting an eye at you?

Well, we can't guarantee the third option, but we're sure that Ampersand is still the place for you!

Ampersand was built on our founder's intense, almost perpetual craving for food of all kinds, especially when some weren't available whenever she wanted them—a quality we're sure that everyone has had at one point in their lives. Here, you can get any of our specialty dishes at any time of day as long as we're open in as many different ways as there are colors of the rainbow!

We strive to provide our diners with the highest quality foods & beverages with the quickest speed & service plus the greatest hospitality & service possible! The customer is always right, & we will do our best to treat you like royalty.
